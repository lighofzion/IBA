Who We Are

We are God's workers to help your life of faith grow in the Word.

International Bible Academy is an online Bible college established through the collaboration of many individuals devoted to theological education. We transcend religious and cultural boundaries in achieving our mission. Time, location, and finances are not barriers as we reach out to anyone who hungers for God's Word and has internet access.

What Our Graduates Say

1. Alex

Studying God's Word at IBA was my first online learning experience, and it was truly transformative. I wasn't particularly engaged with the Bible before, but when a friend invited me to study at IBA, I took the opportunity, especially since it was free. What amazed me most was witnessing my own gradual transformation. Despite my busy work schedule, I prioritized these studies because the Word became precious to me. Each lesson revealed new aspects of God's mystery. God is truly alive! I hope many others will have the opportunity to study at IBA.

2. Christina
